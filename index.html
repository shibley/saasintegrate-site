Programmatic SEO (pSEO) is the practice of generating large numbers of SEO-optimized landing pages from structured data ‚Äî instead of writing each page manually. Think of it as a template + dataset approach: you define the pattern once, then apply it at scale. Instead of 1 page ranking for 1 keyword, you build 200 pages ranking for 200 keywords. The math compounds fast.
Pages are live in under a week. Google typically starts indexing and ranking new pages within 4‚Äì12 weeks, depending on your domain authority. Sites with existing authority tend to rank faster. We submit your sitemap immediately on launch to accelerate crawling.
We typically build with Next.js (or vanilla HTML/JS for simpler projects), deployed on Vercel. Data pipelines use Node.js or Python depending on the source. Everything is structured with JSON-LD schema markup, proper canonical tags, and crawlable HTML ‚Äî no client-side rendering hacks. You get a clean codebase you can hand off to any developer.
